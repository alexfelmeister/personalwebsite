Alex S. Felmeister

I am a first year PhD student at  Drexel University in Information Studies and Informatics at the College of Computing and Informatics.  At Drexel, I work under the advisement of Dr. Tony Hu. My research interests include Translational Biomedical Informatics, scientific software, data driven application development and data integration needed in precision medicine in pediatric rare disease research. As Enterprise Informatics Supervisor at The Children's Hospital of Philadelphia (CHOP), Department of Biomedical and Health Informatics (DBHi), I lead the effort in the development, implementation and integration of the informatics infrastructure designed to support complex biorepositories for both specific translational research projects and cross-institutional efforts. 

From 2008 to 2012, I served as the primary liaison for biomedical informatics and the CHOP Research Institute’s Center for Childhood Cancer Researchwhere I lead the implementation of the Cancer Center’s Clinical Trials Management System (CTMS) and its clinical systems integrations.
