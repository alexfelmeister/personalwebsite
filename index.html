Alex S. Felmeister

Currently, I am completing my doctoral studies at Drexel University in Information Studies and Informatics at the College of Computing and Informatics.  As Enterprise Informatics Supervisor at The Children's Hospital of Philadelphia(CHOP), Department of Biomedical and Health Informatics(DBHi), I lead the effort in the development, implementation and integration of the informatics infrastructure designed to support complex biorepositories for both specific translational research projects and cross-institutional efforts. 

From 2008 to 2012, I served as the primary liaison for biomedical informatics and the CHOP Research Institute’s Center for Childhood Cancer Research where I lead the implementation of the Cancer Center’s Clinical Trials Management System (CTMS) and its clinical systems integrations.

In addition to my post-graduate degree in Information Systems/Database Systems, I received a bachelor's in communications and theater with a minor in finance from Temple University.
