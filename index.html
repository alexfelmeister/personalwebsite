Alex S. Felmeister

Email Me: felmeistera@email.chop.edu

I am a first year, part-time, PhD student at  Drexel University in Information Studies and Informatics at the College of Computing and Informatics where I work under the advisement of Dr. Tony Hu. I am also Enterprise Informatics Supervisor at The Children's Hospital of Philadelphia (CHOP), Department of Biomedical and Health Informatics (DBHi) where I lead the effort in the development, implementation and integration of the informatics infrastructure designed to support complex biorepositories for both specific translational research projects and cross-institutional efforts. 

Though it seems like a full plate, both my academic interests and professional life have a unique synergy. I hope to make strides in my studies and apply them to make a true difference in biomedical research at CHOP, for scientists and patients.

My academic interests include Translational Biomedical Informatics, scientific software integration, data driven application development and genomic and clinical data integration needed in precision medicine for pediatric rare disease research.
